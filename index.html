About Microgreen Growing

Microgreens are tiny plants packed with nutrients and flavor. With this kit, you can grow your own superfood in less than a week. The seed and planting cup are provided for you, making this process simple and fun!

How to Grow

Step 1: Use the provided seed ball. These seeds are selected for fast growth and high nutrition.

Step 2: Place the seed ball in the cup provided in your kit.

Step 3: Fill the cup with soil or cocopeat until it covers the seed ball.

Step 4: Spread seeds evenly and gently press them into the soil.

Step 5: Spray water gently to moisten the soil. Keep it slightly damp, not soggy.

Step 6: Keep the cup in a dark place for the first 2 days, then move it to indirect sunlight. Spray water twice daily.

How It Works

The Microgrow Kit makes it simple for anyone to grow nutrient-rich greens at home. The seed ball contains all necessary nutrients, and the cup ensures proper growth. Just follow the easy steps, and in a week you'll enjoy fresh, healthy microgreens from your own home garden!

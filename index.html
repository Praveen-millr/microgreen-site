Fast Results

Microgreens sprout quickly — great for short demos where visitors can see real progress.
1

Prepare the cup

Place a little growing medium (or damp paper towel) in the cup. If using soil, moisten it lightly so it holds together.

2

Plant the seed ball

Press the seed ball gently into the medium, cover lightly (if required) and mist with water. Keep the cup in indirect light.

3

Care & harvest

Mist twice daily. In 7–10 days, snip microgreens above the soil line. Rinse and enjoy or show visitors the result.

How it works — short explanation

Microgreens are young seedlings of vegetables and herbs. They contain concentrated flavor and nutrients. With a small kit, you can demonstrate the full life-cycle in a week. The demo uses small pre-measured seed balls to improve germination consistency and reduce mess.

Simple care tips

  • Keep medium moist but not waterlogged.
  • Place in bright, indirect light — avoid strong midday sun on the table.
  • Maintain room temperature (20–26°C) for best growth.
  • Label cups so visitors can track different seed types.

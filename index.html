About Microgreen Growing

Microgreens are tiny plants packed with nutrients and flavor. With this kit, you can grow your own superfood in less than a week. The seeds in colorful paper packets and planting cup are provided for you, making this process simple and fun!

How to Grow

Step 1: Open the colorful seed packet. These seeds are chosen for fast growth and high nutrition.

Step 2: Use the planting cup provided in your kit.

Step 3: Fill the cup with soil or cocopeat until it's about 3/4 full.

Step 4: Spread the seeds evenly over the soil and gently press them in.

Step 5: Spray water gently to moisten the soil. Keep it slightly damp, not soggy.

Step 6: Keep the cup in a dark place for the first 2 days, then move it to indirect sunlight. Spray water twice daily.

How It Works

The Microgrow Kit makes it simple for anyone to grow nutrient-rich greens at home. The colorful seed packets and planting cups make this activity easy and fun. Just follow the steps, and in a week you'll enjoy fresh, healthy microgreens right from your table!
